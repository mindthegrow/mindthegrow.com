About Us

Our Mission

At Mind the Grow, we leverage data analytics to help your Agribusiness grow, maintain, and sustain. From seed to store, we are here to advise and consult on agricultural and business decisions.

Democratizing Data-Driven Decision Making

We help your Agribusiness identify the risks it faces and the opportunities it has to grow. No data? No problem. We can still help. We have a rich library of simulations to draw from to make predictions based on feasible ranges for all the uncertainties involved.

Unlike most, we won’t sell you on solutions you don’t need. Our team consists of academics with a vested interest in improving access to technology. We envision a world where small farmers have access to the same cutting-edge planning tools as their largest competitors.

Agricultural Development and Sustainability

At Mind the Grow, we are devoted to sustainability–for the environment and your business. We understand that many operations are family-owned–that cooperatives scrape together resources to ensure their survival. Our focus is assuring that your farm (or Agribusiness) can make long-term decisions to reward your dedication.

Uncertainty and Risk Mitigation

Agribusiness is especially susceptible to uncertainty, risk, and unsustainable growth because success depends on both market trends and factors affecting the farm. Our software products and consultation offerings leverage computational mathematics to quantify this uncertainty, delivering outcomes with flexibility to help navigate an uncertain landscape.

Environmentally conscientious sustainability can still be practiced without compromising profits. Reach out if you have any questions or want to learn how Mind the Grow can help your business develop sustainable practices and ensure multi-generational longevity.
