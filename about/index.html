About Us

Our Mission

At Mind the Grow, we leverage data analytics to help your Agribusiness grow, maintain, and sustain. From seed to store, we are here to advise and consult on agricultural and business decisions.

Data-Driven Decision Making

We help your Agribusiness identify the risks it faces and the opportunities it has to grow. Our proprietary methods yield a flexible set of decisions that lead to success under uncertainty.

No data? No problem. We can still help.

We utilize cutting-edge mathematical and computer-science tools to analyze business patterns, even in the absence of large data reserves. By combining research and field-specific data trends with our proprietary software, we can make projections of future outcomes and consult on better business practices.

Agricultural Development and Sustainability

At Mind the Grow, we are devoted to sustainability–for the environment and your business. Environmentally conscientious sustainability can still be practiced without compromising your profits.

Contact us to learn how Mind the Grow can help your business develop sustainable practices.

Uncertainty Risk Mitigation

Agribusiness is twice as susceptible to uncertainty, risk, and unsustainability because success depends on factors in the market and on the farm. Our products and services utilize computational mathematics to quantify this uncertainty, delivering outcomes with flexible variance to help guide your businesses decisions.

Communication

Everything starts with a conversation. We consider the challenges unique to your business and compile a list of your ideas for potential solutions.

Data is nothing without delivery. We make sure that every document you receive is easily understandable and visually compelling. Our reports serve as excellent materials for potential investors in your company.
