Market Research

Problem

Business owners are too busy managing day-to-day operations to devote any significant amount of time to researching. Decisions about the potential size of markets for new products are difficult to make without an understanding of the influence of estimation. Uncertainties at each step of the research process can compound and lead to conclusions about the potential revenue to be wildly inaccurate.

Approach

We identify the quantities of interest relevant to your specific problem and then hone in on what data to collect. Using our proprietary techniques in optimal experimental design, we are able to save you up to 30% on your research budget by reducing the number of data points necessary. We are able to accomplish this owing to our deep expertise in theoretical and computational mathematics to pinpoint the most informative experiments and surveys. Unlike other firms, we do not rely on data being perfect, rather we incorporate uncertainty into the process to allow for robust decision making.

Outcome

Mind the Grow provides you with a detailed research report that outlines the size of the opportunity we are asked to investigate. The various possible outcomes are presented side-by-side in order to make the data transparent and simple to interpret. You can request that we include any of the following into our analysis:

  • behavior modeling
  • cost-benefit analysis
  • price-sensitivity
  • analysis of variance
  • customer segmentation
