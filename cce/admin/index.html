Administration

REQUIREMENTS

  1. Description of proposed hours of operation. You should review the Lawful Presence Affidavit for more information.

  2. A “Responsible Operations Plan,” which details how “employees will monitor and prevent over-intoxication, underage access to the DCA, driving under the influence of marijuana, the illegal distribution of marijuana or marijuna products within DCA, and any other potential criminal activity on the premises.”

  3. A documented employee training program that addresses all components of the Responsible Operations Plan.

  4. Dates for any planned events that would be conducted as part of the establishment as an extension of normal operations.

  5. For any “Person” owning 5% or more of the entity or receiving 5% or more of profits (as well as any trade names or assumed names), you must submit:

Name, address, email, and date of birth. National criminal history records check conducted by the FBI upon submission of fingerprinting records an all required documents. Suitable evidence of proof of lawful presence.

  1. The Basic Application is available on the Denver government website, along with a FAQ, and Advisement Affidavit, which you will need if someone is preparing the documents on your behalf.
