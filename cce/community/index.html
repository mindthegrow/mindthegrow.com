Community

REQUIREMENTS

  1. For any suitable location, take note of all registered neighborhood organizations (RNO) in the district.

  2. You should receive written approval/endorsement from at least one such eligible organization.

  3. Craft formal plan to create positive impacts in the neighborhood where the licensed premises are located.

The full outline of what constitutes licensing requirements can be found in Sec. 6-210 of the DRMC.

CHALLENGES

  1. Find the organizations with which you will engage to craft a plan for community engagement. Start with an official list of RNOs in Denver. The Community Engagement Plan from the Dept. of Excise and Licenses is a useful document for your search.

  2. Your organization must have written plans to promote and encourage full participation in the regulated marijuana industry in order to positively impact the community.

  3. Have written policies and procedures to address any concerns or complaints expressed by residents and businesses within the neighborhood surrounding the licensed premises.
