Design

REQUIREMENTS

  1. A health and sanitation plan for sanitization and cleaning of cannabis consumption accessories to be rented or otherwise made available for use. Information on Health Inspections can be found on Denver’s government website.

  2. A marijuana waste plan that includes a detailed description of how employees will dispose of any Waste that is left, abandoned, or otherwise not consumed on the premises.

  3. An odor control and abatement plan in the same form and substance as would be required in DRMC Sec 4-10, and any rules promulgated thereto, if the applicant intends to allow smoking or vaping of marijuana within the Designated Consumption Area (DCA).

  4. A red-lined floor plan of the DCA showing its location within the business. See below for details.

  5. A description and supporting evidence that details how the DCA complies with the Colorado Clean Indoor Air Act.

A Public Health and Sanitation Basic Questionnaire available through the Dept. of Public Health & Environment is an appropriate place to start.

DETAILS

  1. Draw a floor plan to-scale on an 8.5x11 piece of paper, one page per floor. It must show the layout of the establishment and the principal uses of each section of the floor area.

  2. The location of the DCA within the establishment must be contiguous and outlined in red.

  3. Include dimensions, security cameras, and establishment boundaries, including external and internal walls, doors, fences, gates and the like.

  4. Indicate where all signage will be located. These should cover responsible usage, access restrictions, and any standardized placards. As with all buildings, exit and emergency signage should also be shown.

  5. You must specify where you will be locating your posting notice for the public hearing.
