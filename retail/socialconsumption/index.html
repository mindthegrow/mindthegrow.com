Social Consumption License

Problem

A client approached us with interest in exploring the feasibility of opening up a new Cannabis Consumption Enterprise in Denver, CO (our home-base!).

The passing of Initiative 300 to legalize designated areas for the consumption of cannabis products was exciting, but the legal hoops to jump through are numerous, so it is no surprise that there is a severe paucity of operational businesses. At the time of this writing (mid-2018), only two are operational.

Our client felt they could do much better. We at Mind the Grow can help them execute.

Approach

The client wanted a comprehensive overview of the process and a cost-of-entry analysis in order to assess whether or not to proceed with such an investment. Since there would be a long period of applying, they would need to sustain themselves and the lease for an undetermined amount of time.

We determined the range of costs that would be faced in order to allow the client to know how much external investment would be required should they choose to open the business.

As part of our research and presentation to the client, we categorized the hurdles to overcome into logical groupings. Then we decided to share this with the world. We want to see more enterprises and competition in the mix.

Outcome

The consultants at Mind the Grow help un-muddy the waters by providing a cohesive summary of the requirements, restrictions, and procedures that surround opening a new Cannabis Consumption Enterprise. Check out the digital write-up we created as a supplement
