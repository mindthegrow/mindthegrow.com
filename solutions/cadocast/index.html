Avocados Today

Avocados are trending. Sales have grown tremendously over the past decade due to factors such as cuisine trends (avocado toast) and diet crazes (Paleo and KETO diets). But it is not merely fads that drive sales. The Avocado is a vital staple of the North American diet. Thousands of fast-casual restaurants and Tuesday night taco dinners rely on the creamy fruit to supply flavor and nutrients. The forecast clearly demonstrates that sales will only grow higher.

Avocado orchards and farms are hard pressed to output a consistent, quality product. However, production rates depend heavily upon an array of factors, ranging anywhere from pruning, planting, and watering practices to climate change and disease exposure. We account for uncertainty to predict future outcomes and asses the underlying risks in agricultural operations of all sizes.

CadoCast

Mind the Grow offers CadoCast, a software solution that analyzes the factors influencing Avocado agribusiness. CadoCast utilizes existing data, supplementary models, and Avocado industry-specific factors to create simulations of future production trends. By analyzing these trends and comparing them with operation procedures, CadoCast guides better business practice through data-driven decision making.
