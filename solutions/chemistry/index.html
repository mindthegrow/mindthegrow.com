Quality Assurance, Extraction, and Testing

Precision

Testing equipment has some natural variation built in to it that scientists are used to handling. However, classical statistical methods fail to address the natural variability present in different batches of product.

The approach we take is rooted in cutting-edge computational mathematics to maximize the utility of information and address the underlying uncertainty in potency inherent to the cannabis plants.

Efficiency

We can use the latest developments from simulation-based optimal experimental design to adapt your testing regiments to budgetary and temporal constraints. By identifying the most useful information to gather, we save time, effort, and money by minimizing the number of trials you have to perform to reduce the uncertainty in your estimates.
